注意事項：
1. 平均成績計算方式為：學期總分/修習學分數=(學分數x每一科目成績)/修習學分數
Semester Average Score：sum of (credits x score) / total credits Chosen.
2. 暑修的成績不會列入該學期的平均成績計算當中，僅會計算在畢業的總平均成績之中。
The score on any summer-session courses will be calculated into the graduation grade average, not into the semester average score.
3. 「成績排名」是暫時的排名，僅提供同學參考。「正確的成績排名」須等到次學期開學時，班上的所有同學成績都到齊以後才會正確，請屆時再提出成績排名的申請。
"Accumulated Ranking" is a temporary ranking, only for students for reference. You may apply for the exact ranking after a new semester begins. Graduate students in the second year of study and abovewill not be included in the calculation because each have different areas of specialization.
4. G.P.A的等級點數換算表如下，G.P.A的計算方式：(學分數 × 點數)的總和 / 總學分。
G.P.A= sum of (credits x grade point) / total credits. The dissertation score is excluded in GPA.
5. 「W」表示課程停修。 W: Course Withdrawn
